ubugari recipe

how to make Ubugari

ubugari is an eastern/wester African stable dish that is made from grounded cassava that is then mixed with water to have strong consistency usually eaten with a stew .

Ingredients

  • cassava flour
  • water

steps to make ubugari

  1. bring a pot full water to a boil
  2. when the water has boiled reduce it and leave just about half of the pot
  3. add four cups of cassava flour and let it sit on top of the water for a minute
  4. mix the cassava with the water with a wooden spatula until u reach a hard consistency
  5. keep mixing until all the flour no more flour is visible if needed add water and keep mixing
  6. close the pot and keep it a lowest heat for about a minute and half
  7. turn off the stove and remove the pot now you have delicious pot of ubugari
  8. pair it with a stew of your choice and enjoy
