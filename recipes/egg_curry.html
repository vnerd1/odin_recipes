egg curry recipe

how to make egg curry

this is an endian dish key steps involve frying spices (cumin, bay leaf), sautéing onions and ginger-garlic, cooking down tomatoes with powders (turmeric, chili, coriander), and simmering with water and eggs for 5-10 minutes

Ingredients

  • boiled eggs (shelled and slit)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes, pureed or finely chopped
  • <1 tsp cumin seeds/li>
  • Spices: 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup water
  • salt to taste

    • steps to making egg currry

    1. put a bit of oil in a panand add tumeric and chill(if you want them spicy)sear the eggs until they turn golden brown
    2. add abit oil in a different pan and add all the spices and add an onion an saute until golden brown
    3. add ginger-garlic paste and saute for two minutes add tomatoes ,tumeric,chillipowder,coiander powder and salt cook until the mixtue thickens
    4. add 2 cups of water and reduce heat let it simmer for 10 minutes
    5. add the eggs and let it simmer for nother 6 minutes
    6. add in galam masala and garnish with coriander
    7. serve your dish with rice and enjoy
