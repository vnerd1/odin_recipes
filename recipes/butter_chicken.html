butter chicken recipe

how to make butter chicken

This classic, creamy Indian indian dish features tender, marinated chicken simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce, finished with butter, cream, and aromatic spices. Key elements include a 1-hour yoghurt marinade, pan-searing or grilling the chicken, and a blended, silky tomato-cashew sauce, topped with crushed fenugreek leaves

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (500g) boneless/skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • ½ cup plain, unsweetened Greek yoghurt
  • lemon juice
  • ginger-garlic paste (grated)
  • garam masala
  • Kashmiri chili powder (or paprika)
  • ground turmeric
  • salt
  • butter+oil
  • onion
  • ground cumin
  • tomato puree/passata
  • heavy cream
  • sugar
  • blended cashew
  • cilanto

steps to making butter chicken

  1. mainate chicken in a bowl by adding greek yoghurt lemon,ginger_garlic paste ,garam masala,kashmiri chili powder and ground tumeic and let it set for atleast an hour
  2. heat some oil in a pan and sear the chicken till it turns golden brown and set the chicken aside
  3. in the same pan melt some butter and add onion and saute until soft
  4. add the spices (ginger_garlic paste,cumin,garam masal and chillipowder)
  5. add in the tomato puree and suga and let it simmer on low heat until sauce thickens
  6. add cashew puree and the chicken and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes
  7. add in extra butter if preffered and top with cilantro
  8. serve with rice o naan
